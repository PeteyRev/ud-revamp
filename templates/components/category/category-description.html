Eco Desktop

The Eco desktop offers beauty and resilience in a sustainable package. This top is made from 100 percent recycled wood that is certified by the Sustainable Forestry Initiative. The recycled wood comes from saw mills, so it not only saves trees, but also reduces waste in landfills. The wood is then shaped with a CNC machine to smooth the edges. Then, our Missouri plant applies an eco-friendly, UV-cured powder coat, which offers a durable surface that is more resilient than paint.
