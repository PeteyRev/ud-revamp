Customize it

We've got more customization possibilities than anyone else in the industry, hands down. Choose from three base colors and add the top and accessories of your choice to create your customized workstation.

A multitude of materials

Custom laminate: Our laminate maker will custom-make your top to order, using your choice of Wilsonart's extensive color and pattern selections, all GREENGUARD-certified. There are thousands of additional colors and patterns available. If you need a center cutout or an unusual shape, no problem.

Reclaimed wood: Because the wood was originally cut from timber that had been growing for hundreds of years, these tops have a unique character, with more complex growth-ring pattern than you find on newer woods.

Bamboo: Our 1" thick option gives you a beautiful desk at an exceptional price. You can also upgrade to our premium bamboo, featuring a thickness of 1.5", a richer grain with more nuanced color, a vertical grain core, and chamfered edges.

Solid Wood: Our wide selection of solid wood styles are handmade to order in a variety of standard and custom sizes. These top-of-the-line desks give your office unmatched beauty and character.

Amazing Accessories

You can further customize your desk by adding accessories according to your preferences. We especially recommend a monitor arm for quick and easy adjustments, a wire management kit to keep your desk organized, and a desk mat for extra-comfortable standing.
