UPLIFT Desk Power & Data Link

If your office consists of several desks or more, how do you power them all? You can easily plug them into the wall if each desk is close to a power outlet, but if desks are laid out in a line that goes perpendicular to the wall, getting adequate power to every desk can be quite the task.

Happy and healthy people are the foundation of a productive workplace, just like the foundation of every electric height adjustable desk is a proper power source. Enjoy more freedom to adjust to your sitting and standing heights with the UPLIFT Desk Power & Data Link.

The Power & Data Link delivers reliable power to groups of sit-to-stand desks, providing a well-powered and flexible office setup for you and your employees.

Flexible Office Power & Data Link from UPLIFT Desk

Neighboring desks will have a unifying under-the-desk 6 prong vessel that powers their height adjustable desks with no problem. Enjoy a well-powered workstation without an unsightly trail of cords leading to the nearest power outlet.

Beginning with the main power system that's installed to the first desk, power is passed on through a linked system to remaining neighboring desks via a looping cable, delivering reliable power to grouped workstations.

Initial power is supplied to groups of desks via a 3 prong 120 Volt single circuit plug, or directly wired to your power source, up to 4 circuits, depending on your office’s power capabilities. Each linked group has a 20 Amp max limit per circuit. Depending on the electrical draw of the equipment and gear at your desk, you can typically attach 4 desks to a single 120 Volt circuit. By combining circuits you can do longer desk runs of up to 16 desks on a 4 circuit wall plug.

The power system mounts directly underneath each desk and comes with 6 - 120 Volt power receptacles, 2 - 3.6 Amp USB charging ports, 1 - telecom plate knockout, and mounting hardware.
